Our ultrafast self‑driving lab (SDL) combines femtosecond lasers, programmable optics, and machine‑learning‑driven autonomy to discover and optimize ultrafast light–matter interactions. The platform closes the loop between hypothesis, experiment selection, data acquisition, and interpretable model discovery.
